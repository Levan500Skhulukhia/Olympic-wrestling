Greco-Roman Wrestling

Greco-Roman wrestling is a style of wrestling that forbids holds below the waist. It is one of the two styles of wrestling contested in the Olympic Games, along with freestyle wrestling. The sport places a high emphasis on upper-body strength and technique.
